Calculator/Parser

Overview

Can parse a mathematical equation into an abstract binary tree representation

In addition the application has the ability to perform operations such as calculating a value, slope at a point, derivative, Taylor/MacLaurin series, and definite integrals

Can also Graph the equation given its tree
